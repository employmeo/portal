To get started, tell us about the position you are hiring for

Next, select how you will benchmark your employees.

Choosing "Simple" will provide a unique link that you can send to all of your employees.

Choosing "Performance" will provide two unique links. One to send to only your top performing employees. The second, to send to all of the rest.

Choosing "Detailed" allows you to provide a list of employees, mark them as as top performer, and follow up to ensure they have completed the assessment.

Please prepare a CSV (comma separated) file, with a list of the employees you wish to include. The file should have a header row, and these four columns:

  • firstName - Employee First name
  • lastName - Employee Last name
  • email - Employee email address
  • topPerformer - True / False indication if employee is one of top performers

Column Headers are Case Sensitive.
