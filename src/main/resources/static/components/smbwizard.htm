Simple is the easiest way to get going. We will give you a link to send to at least 10 employees. After they take the test, we will give you back a detailed analysis of how your employees rank against the general population.

Choosing Top Performers will give you 2 links, one to send to your top performers and one to send to the rest of your team. It is best with at least 10 people in each group. Once everyone takes the test, you will get back a detailed analysis of the factors that matter most in predicting your top performers, and you will have your own custom model for scoring new applicants.

Custom Predictions lets you decide what the system predicts. You will need to upload a .csv file with the names and email addresses of all the employees you want to take this test, and a yes or no column if they are a top performer. For more predictions, just contact us at the number below, and we can work with you on them.
